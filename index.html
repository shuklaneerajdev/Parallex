Life is a continuos result of past experiences, our ability to interpret them as well as our willingness to throw ourselves in new situations that might not be totally in our control. Dr. Seuss

Traveling is one of the ways to put ourselves out in the open, on the mercy of the new situations and hence a test of our ability to navigate through them and thereby enhance and expand our world.

Over all, we should expose our senses to new feelings. While at the same time, we should ensure that we have a control over what we expose our senses to. Dr. Seuss Again.

Bob Hope
